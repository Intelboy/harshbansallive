About Me

With over 10 years of teaching experience, I am passionate about empowering students to excel in their careers through education, mentorship, and hands-on training. Currently, I serve as an Assistant Professor at SPSU, specializing in Blockchain, RPA (Robotic Process Automation), Programming and Algorithm Design.

I have had the privilege of providing in-house training for companies like Virtusa and Tech Mahindra, preparing students to meet industry standards. Over the past four years, I have conducted certification training on the Automation Anywhere RPA tool and Blue Prism, enabling students to acquire valuable, job-ready skills in automation.

Additionally, I have a strong track record in placement preparation, helping students refine their technical and interview skills. As a core member of the Kargil Batch at Chandigarh University, I was one of five trainers responsible for preparing students for placements in both product-based and service-based companies for four years.

My mentorship has also led teams to achieve national recognition, including guiding 3 teams to victory in the Smart India Hackathon 2023 and 2024 at the national level.

Currently pursuing my Ph.D. in Computer Science and Engineering from Chandigarh University, focusing on a Blockchain-Based Secure Framework for Enhanced Educational Ecosystem.

Driven by a commitment to lifelong learning, I aim to bridge the gap between academic knowledge and industry application, ensuring my students are equipped with the skills and mindset to thrive in the rapidly evolving tech landscape.

Let's connect and collaborate on initiatives that push the boundaries of innovation and education!
