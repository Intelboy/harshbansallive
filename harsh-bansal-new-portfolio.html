About Me

I am an Assistant Professor with over 10 years of experience in academia and industry collaboration. Currently working at SPSU, Udaipur, I specialize in Blockchain, RPA (Robotic Process Automation), and Programming with a focus on Data Structures and Algorithms.

My expertise includes mentoring national-winning teams for hackathons, developing industry-relevant curriculum, and bridging the gap between academic theory and practical applications. As a national-level mentor for Smart India Hackathon, I've guided multiple teams to victory by addressing real-world challenges posed by government organizations.

I am passionate about enhancing educational ecosystems through technology, which is reflected in my Ph.D. research on Blockchain-based secure frameworks for education. My teaching philosophy centers on equipping students with both theoretical knowledge and practical skills that are directly applicable in industry settings.

In addition to my academic role, I have extensive experience in RPA development and training, having collaborated with companies like Virtusa and Tech Mahindra to deliver specialized training programs for industry professionals.
